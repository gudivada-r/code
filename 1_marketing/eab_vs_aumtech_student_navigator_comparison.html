The Marketing Hook

The Aumtech Differentiators

  • We Don't Manage Logistics, We Enable Learning: EAB is an administrative tool disguised as a student app. Aumtech actually helps students pass classes with AI Flashcards, autonomous syllabus scanning, and real-time AI Insights.
  • Zero-Liability Data Architecture: Say goodbye to massive data compliance headaches. Aumtech operates as a stateless proxy over the EdNex warehouse. If a student deletes the app, their data never lived on our servers anyway.
  • Speaking the Gen Z / Alpha Language: We abandoned institutional jargon. Students don't "Submit a Tier 1 Help Request," they complete a "Solo Quest" or "Tag In An Advisor" when they need backup.
  • Proactive, Not Reactive: EAB tells you a hold exists. Get Aura tells you how to fix it, who to email, and drafts the email for you.
Deployed Features

New Features Deployed to Beat EAB

  • "Panic Button" / Instant Hand Raise: A highly visible, one-click triage button (e.g., "I Need Help Now") instantly connects a student to crisis counselors, financial emergency aid, and drops them into an emergency Chat queue.
  • AI-Powered Career & Major Mapping: EAB relies on basic Major Explorer quizzes. Aumtech integrates Labor Market Data into our AI so "Get Aura" visually maps out step-by-step career pathways based on their current credits.
  • Intelligent Calendar Sync (Push): Rather than just pulling data, we automatically inject Syllabus Scanner output (Deadlines) directly into the student's native Apple/Google Calendar via an ICS subscription feed.
  • Targeted "Nudges" via SMS/Push: Real-time proactive alerts powered by background jobs. If a student's GPA dips below a threshold, the system automatically pushes an intervention message instantly.
